Glenforest FIRST Robotics Team '6070'

Coding Team

In my final year of high school, I was lucky enough to be apart of my school's FIRST Robotics Team as a programmer! I worked alongside 15 to 20 of my peers to design and develop our robot's program to do the tasks it required.

This was an amazing learning experience as I was able to collaborate with other programmers, and I got to see first hand what goes into coding a robot!

Nahani Way Robotics

Lead Mentor

I was a lead mentor for Nahani Way Robotics, a program set up by the STEM club at my school to teach elementary school students the basics of robotics through Lego Robotics.

I was able to create and demonstrate lessons for these students, teaching them the base foundations of logic and math required to program and build a Lego Robot.
